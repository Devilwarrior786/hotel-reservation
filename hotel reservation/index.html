About the Hotel
★5Star Resort
A Legacy of Impeccable Hospitality

The Serene Grand is a five-star luxury resort perched at 3,500 ft amid the coffee-scented mist of Coorg. Our 8-acre estate blends colonial architecture with contemporary comforts, offering each guest an intimate escape from the everyday.

From our heated infinity pool overlooking the Western Ghats to our award-winning Ayurvedic spa, every detail is curated to exceed expectation.

Our Location

Nestled at Madikeri, Kodagu District — the heart of Coorg — The Serene Grand is easily accessible from Bangalore (265 km) and Mysore (118 km). We sit at an elevation of 3,500 ft offering panoramic views of the Western Ghats.
